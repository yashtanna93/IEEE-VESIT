Melange

IEEE - VESIT: EXCELLENCE THROUGH INNOVATION


Melange is the All-Bombay Symposium of IEEE-VESIT Student Branch. Melange means Confluence. It is a technical conference that is aimed at engineering students who want to learn more about the finer technical aspects. Technology today is reaching dizzying heights. The focus today is to encourage research to develop cutting-edge yet cost-effective technologies. The challenge is to bring about an awareness among the youth of technology and thus open the doors of this journey to them. This is exactly what we have in mind as we set out to give this challenge a direction and a meaningful course. Melange is but a tool, endeavoring to demonstrate to our valued student members, this new world. It is a convergence-a forum to bring together ideas and technologies from all fields of engineering. Last year, as every year the auditorium was filled to capacity with 250 members . All our members, successful in our various event are felicitated and our Chairperson addressed the audience about the vision and achievements of IEEE-VESIT.
